Jollof Rice Recipe

Description

Jollof rice, or "party jollof" is a one pot, tomato and pepper infused rice with a layer of crispy rice at the bottom

It is loved by most Nigerians and it is a dish that you would definitely be served in any Nigerian party

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • ½ large onion, diced
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 3 cups parboiled rice
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, corn, peas)

Steps

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-low heat and cook 1/2 onion until soft and translucent but not browned, about 5 minutes.

  2. Add tomato sauce; cook and stir until slightly thickened and infused into the oil, 5 to 7 minutes.

  3. Stir coconut milk, 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence, salt, and pepper into the pot.

  4. Bring to a simmer; add rice.Cook, stirring often, until rice is almost tender, 15 to 20 minutes.

  5. Add frozen vegetables and continue cooking until rice is tender and creamy, about 5 minutes.
